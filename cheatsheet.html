How It Works

2-Pane Layout

Every workspace starts with terminal (70%) + lazygit (30%). Lazygit auto-spawns on startup and when switching worktrees.

Workspaces

Each worktree gets its own workspace. Switch with ⌘⇧[ and ⌘⇧]. Tabs and panes persist per workspace.

AI Sessions

Picker shows AI status: 🟢 Claude, 🟡 Codex, 🔵 OpenCode. Uses lsof to detect working directory.

AI Status (Always Visible in Status Bar)
🟢 Claude running
🟡 Codex running
🔵 OpenCode running
○ No AI session
